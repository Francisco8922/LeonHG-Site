About

Your professional partners.

Leon Hospitality Group was founded by Francisco "Cisco" Peralta, a hospitality and operations leader with 15+ years managing premium residential, hospitality, and service-driven environments.

Leon helps landlords, property managers, and real estate operators turn underutilized units into professionally managed furnished housing assets. With a focus on corporate housing, extended-stay demand, guest experience, and owner peace of mind, Leon brings hotel-grade care, operational oversight, and reliable communication to every property partnership.

Cisco brings a hands-on understanding of what owners care about most: predictable performance, screened guests, well-maintained spaces, and operations that don't require their constant attention.

Leon was built for owners who want stronger rental performance without taking on another job. The mission is straightforward: protect the asset, elevate the experience, and make furnished housing operations feel seamless.
