About

Your professional partners.

Leon Hospitality Group was founded by Francisco "Cisco" Peralta, a hospitality and operations leader with 15+ years running high-touch spaces for demanding clients.

Cisco currently serves as General Manager at Elite Amenity Management at 50 West, an ultra-luxury residential tower in the Financial District, and has led operations at Bond Collective's flagship coworking location since 2021.

Leon was built to bring that same standard of care, design, and operational discipline to property owners and operators who want their investments to feel like assets again, not second jobs.
